Automated exam correction system

This site aimed at designing an electronic correction system to correct students exams. The exams can be pictures, pdf or office files that contain texts,
The exam will be corrected and graded by being compared to a given typical answer (model answer).
The system shows and corrects students' errors as well as determining the student level. The system also gives the pass and fail percentages and other details needed for the evaluation process.
